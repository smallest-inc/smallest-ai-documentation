## Manage subscription

The **Explore Plans** tab shows the available plans and marks your current one as **CURRENT**. To change plans, pick a plan and click **Upgrade** or **Downgrade Plan**.

The **Billing & Invoices** tab shows:

| Section | What's there |
|---------|--------------|
| **Payment Method** | Card on file. Added charges, auto-recharge credits, and invoices are billed to this card. |
| **Invoices** | All / Pending / Paid filters over your invoice history |

To add or change your card, click **Add Payment Method** on the Billing & Invoices tab.

## Plans

Two plans: **Pay As You Go** and **Enterprise**. Pay As You Go starts with $10 in free credits and full access. Enterprise is quoted per-team based on volume, SLA, and compliance needs.

### Overview

| | Pay As You Go | Enterprise |
|---|---|---|
| **Best for** | Builders, pilots, and small-scale deployments. No monthly commitment. | Production workloads at scale. Reliability, security, and dedicated support. |
| **Number of agents** | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| **Free credits** | $10 to start | Custom |
| **Getting started** | [Sign up](https://app.smallest.ai/login) | [Contact sales](https://smallest.ai/book-a-demo) |

### Price per call

Depends on the architecture you choose. **Cascading** stacks (Hosting + STT + LLM + TTS + Telephony) bill each layer separately; **speech-to-speech** stacks (Hosting + S2S + Telephony) bill the S2S layer as one line.

| | Pay As You Go | Enterprise |
|---|---|---|
| **Price per call** | $0.09 – $0.21 / min (depending on models selected) | As low as $0.05 / min |
| **Smallest Hosting fees** | $0.01 / min, flat rate | Custom |
