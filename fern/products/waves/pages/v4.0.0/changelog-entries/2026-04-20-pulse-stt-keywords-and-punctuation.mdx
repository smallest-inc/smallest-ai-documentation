---
title: Pulse STT keyword boosting and punctuation controls
---

Pulse STT now accepts `punctuate` and `capitalize` query parameters on the WebSocket handshake. Both default to `true`. Set to `false` for domains where consistent post-processing is preferred.

Keyword boosting documentation now describes the supported INTENSIFIER range (0–20, recommended 6). Values above 10 increase the risk of hallucinated keyword matches.
