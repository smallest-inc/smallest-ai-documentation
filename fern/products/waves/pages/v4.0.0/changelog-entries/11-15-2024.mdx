## Introducing Waves

[Waves](https://waves.smallest.ai) is our new unified platform for speech synthesis. It supports various models designed for real-time applications such as voicebots.

Waves will also be supporting various features such as voice cloning, accent cloning, style transfer, speech to speech etc in the near future, making it a ubiquitous platform for anyone who needs AI generated speech.

The voices available on Waves platform are available via the Waves API.
