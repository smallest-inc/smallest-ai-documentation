---
title: Pulse STT Beta tag on South Indian streaming languages
---

Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, and Malayalam (plus the `multi-south-indic` aggregator) are now marked **Beta** on the [Pulse model card](/models/model-cards/speech-to-text/pulse) and the [STT overview](/models/documentation/speech-to-text-pulse/overview). Accuracy improvements are ongoing. These codes remain India-region only (`wss://api.smallest.ai/waves/v1/stt/live`); the US endpoint continues to reject them with `LANGUAGE_NOT_ENABLED_IN_REGION`.

Also cleaned up stale `multi` (bare) language references in the pulse-stt-ws-overrides.yml enum, the pulse-stt API reference, and the voice-cloning-openapi.yaml language example. `multi` was replaced by the current `multi-eu` / `multi-asian` / `north_indic` / `multi-south-indic` aggregators when the streaming enum was revised. Pre-recorded aggregators (`multi-eu`, `multi-asian`) are unchanged.
